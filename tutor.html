Marking Scheme (Key Points)

  • CO₂ transport: buffering pH, increased carrying capacity
  • Cold: many strains; Measles: lifelong immunity
  • Blood A + B → agglutination & haemolysis
  • AB donors: A, B, AB, O (no antibodies)
  • Veins: valves
  • Defect: atherosclerosis / stroke / heart attack
  • Capillaries: thin walls, narrow lumen, slow flow, large surface
  • Low blood volume: transfusion / saline
  • Carboxyhaemoglobin: blocks oxygen transport
  • Pulmonary arteriole: low O₂, high CO₂; venule opposite
