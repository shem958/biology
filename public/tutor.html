Marking Scheme (Key Points)

  • Capillaries: thin walls, narrow lumen, large surface area, slow flow
  • Veins: valves prevent backflow
  • Double circulation: blood passes heart twice per cycle
  • Left ventricle: pumps blood at high pressure
  • Pulmonary artery: low O₂, high CO₂
  • Pulmonary vein: high O₂, low CO₂
  • Carboxyhaemoglobin: prevents oxygen transport
  • Low blood volume: transfusion / saline infusion
